Meta

Meta, formerly known as Facebook, is a technology conglomerate led by CEO Mark Zuckerberg. Renowned for its social media origins, Meta has evolved into a global leader in the development of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) technologies. The company envisions a future where people connect and collaborate seamlessly in the digital realm. Meta continues to invest heavily in innovative technologies, positioning itself as a major player in shaping the next generation of online experiences.
